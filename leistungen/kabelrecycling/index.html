Service for the Berlin region

What this service includes

Our cable recycling service provides environmentally friendly solutions for all types of cables. Whether you have electrical cables, data cables, or industrial wiring, we ensure proper recycling and recovery of valuable materials throughout the Berlin region.

  • Recycling of all cable types: electrical, data, and industrial cables
  • Certified and environmentally compliant recycling process
  • Collection service available or direct delivery to our facility
  • Recovery of valuable metals: copper, aluminium, and other materials
  • Fair pricing based on cable type and metal content

Service Facts

  • Region: Berlin and surroundings
  • Suitable for: private, business, industry
  • Typical project size: small to large
  • Response time: 24-48 hours

How this service works

The process is straightforward. In just a few steps we clarify your requirements, schedule cable recycling and complete the job on site.

1

Contact us

Call us, write via WhatsApp or send a contact form request and briefly describe your cables, location and timing.

2

We plan cable recycling

Together we agree on the exact scope of cable recycling, possible collection or delivery, access on site and the desired date.

3

Execution & settlement

We carry out cable recycling on the agreed date, weigh relevant materials and settle based on fair daily rates or fixed prices.

Who this service is for

Cable recycling is suitable for a wide range of customers. Here are some typical examples - if your situation is different, simply contact us and we will find a solution.

Private households

Ideal for disposing of old electrical cables, extension cords, or cables from home renovations.

Businesses & industry

For ongoing cable waste, production changes, or one-off disposal of electrical and data cables.

Electrical companies

Suitable for electrical contractors, installers, and companies with regular cable waste from projects.

IT & data centers

For data centers, IT companies, and telecommunications businesses disposing of network and data cables.
